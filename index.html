Welcome

This is the official website for a game called Magimals Toxic. Magimals Toxic is a fanmade game in development that I am the Lead Coder for. Though the Toxic group currently is small, this website is made to gain interest and popularity.

The Mission

Our goal

Magimals Toxic's goal is to encourage game developers to keep pace, as even an individual can make a game.


The Game

The Idea

Magimals Toxic is a monster catching game planned to comprise of 4 islands: Montacia Island, Starter Island, Final Bossfight Island, etc. It introduces unique creatures and even a Magimal Superhero Team!
