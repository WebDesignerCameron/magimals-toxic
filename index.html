Welcome

This is the official website for a game called Pokemon Toxic. Pokemon Toxic is a fanmade game in development that I am the Lead Coder for. Though the Toxic group currently is small, this website is made to gain interest and popularity.

The Mission

Our goal

Pokemon Toxic's goal is to encourage game developers to keep pace, as even an individual can make a game.


The Game

The Idea

Pokemon Toxic is a fanmade Pokemon spinoff planned to comprise of 4 islands: Montacia Island, Starter Island, Final Bossfight Island, etc. It introduces all-new Pokemon and even a Pokemon Superhero Team!
